DO I HAVE TO WIN?

Given the choice to win against someone worse than me or lose to someone better, I'll always choose the latter. Because then there's a challenge. And I play to have fun. And try strategies most won't.

META

I do not play the game just to follow what everyone else is doing because it's what's winning/best. If you challenge the meta, you can change it.

PAY2WIN, PAY2WIN-NOW, and PAY2PLAY

Pay2Win:Not all available content is provided with the released title. This is going to diminish a game's community. Pay2Win-Right-Now:The free option of grinding to upgrade is available. Look at Diablo III. I grinded, and farmed in order to get gear so I could solo Diablo on Inferno. That's one way of playing it. With the introduction of the real money auction house, players could just buy whatever items they wanted for a quick, casual game. Same thing for the current raged about game, Hearthstone. Yes, you can use cash to buy packs. You can also use gold. But note: you still have to open the packs. Spending money doesn't increase your odds. Pay2Play:The most current example I can think of is Evolve. The game is $60. But if you want the additional hunters, it's $25. And the monsters are $15 per. That's absurd.
